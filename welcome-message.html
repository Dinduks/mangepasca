The goal of nomzsavior is to provide a way to:

  • Track your groceries
  • Know when a product will expire
  • Know what to eat first

Thus saving the environment and keeping your wallet full.

nomzsavior works offline. Just bookmark it and you're ready to use it anytime you need it!
Data is stored on your browser; clearing cookies for the $URL domain will lead to losing your groceries information.

This app wasn't tested on every single mobile device, which means it may contain bugs. Please feel free to send a pull request or report issues on the GitHub repo.
